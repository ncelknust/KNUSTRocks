Ghana ERW Suitability — Technical Siting Tool

Where in Ghana is enhanced rock weathering most suitable, and why?

This map scores all 216 districts on a 0–100 ERW Suitability Index (ERW-SI), built from three equally auditable drivers on real national data (Geological Survey of Ghana geology, SoilGrids soils, WorldClim climate):

Soil (S)
Restoration need — acidic, low-fertility soils gain most from ERW.
Climate (C)
Rainfall & heat that drive feedstock dissolution.
Geology (G)
Availability of reactive silicate feedstock.

ERW-SI = 40%·S + 30%·C + 30%·G by default — you can re-weight it live.
